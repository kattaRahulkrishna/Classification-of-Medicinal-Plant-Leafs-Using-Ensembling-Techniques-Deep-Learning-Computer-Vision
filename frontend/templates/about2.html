About Us

This project addresses the need for efficient, accurate medicinal plant identification by leveraging advanced deep learning techniques. Automating classification with CNN, MobileNet, and hybrid models enhances precision and accessibility, crucial for researchers and practitioners in traditional medicine, ultimately improving the efficiency and reliability of plant identification.

.

Our study combines CNN, MobileNet, and hybrid models to achieve top-notch accuracy and efficiency in classifying 40 medicinal plant species.
